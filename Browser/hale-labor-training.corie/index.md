## About
This course is open for anyone who wants to learn how to properly handle their kit. Join Mike Hale's academy for labor systems training. Over the course of two, four, eight or twelve weeks, depending on your level and the course selection, you will learn techniques, you will learn safety, you will learn discipline and you will learn how to get the job done. Mike's been running field and simulation training for years. Small classes available weekly, private one-on-ones and discounted private group bookings available. Equipment not provided, sold in-store if needed.
